NASCA Developer Resources

The Native American South Carolina Archive is an open-source project developed by Digital US South at the University of South Carolina. Almost all of These resources are openly provided to anyone who wishes to use them. Some resources, now or in the future, may be password protected. These will be intended only for the future maintainers of the site. A gift, from the coders, just for them, and probably not for the general public.
